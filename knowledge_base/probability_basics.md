# Probability: Basics

## Definitions
- Sample space: set of all outcomes.
- Event: subset of sample space.
- \( P(A) = \frac{\text{number of favourable outcomes}}{\text{total outcomes}} \) (equally likely outcomes).

## Axioms
- \( 0 \leq P(A) \leq 1 \)
- \( P(\text{sample space}) = 1 \)
- \( P(A \cup B) = P(A) + P(B) - P(A \cap B) \)

## Conditional probability
- \( P(A|B) = \frac{P(A \cap B)}{P(B)} \) when \( P(B) > 0 \)
- Bayes: \( P(A|B) = \frac{P(B|A)P(A)}{P(B)} \)

## Independence
- \( A \) and \( B \) independent iff \( P(A \cap B) = P(A)P(B) \).
