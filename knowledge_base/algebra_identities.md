# Algebra: Identities and Formulas

## Quadratic
- \( (a \pm b)^2 = a^2 \pm 2ab + b^2 \)
- \( a^2 - b^2 = (a-b)(a+b) \)
- Roots of \( ax^2 + bx + c = 0 \): \( x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a} \)
- Discriminant \( D = b^2 - 4ac \): D > 0 two real roots, D = 0 repeated root, D < 0 no real roots.

## Exponents and logarithms
- \( a^m \cdot a^n = a^{m+n} \), \( (a^m)^n = a^{mn} \)
- \( \log_a(xy) = \log_a x + \log_a y \), \( \log_a(x/y) = \log_a x - \log_a y \)
- \( \log_a x^n = n \log_a x \)

## Sequences and series
- AP: \( n \)th term \( a_n = a + (n-1)d \), sum \( S_n = \frac{n}{2}(2a + (n-1)d) \)
- GP: \( n \)th term \( a_n = ar^{n-1} \), sum \( S_n = a\frac{1-r^n}{1-r} \) for \( r \neq 1 \)
- Infinite GP sum when \( |r| < 1 \): \( S = \frac{a}{1-r} \)
