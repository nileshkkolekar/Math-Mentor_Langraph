# Calculus: Limits

## Definitions
- \( \lim_{x \to c} f(x) = L \): as \( x \) approaches \( c \), \( f(x) \) approaches \( L \).
- Limit exists only if left and right limits exist and are equal.

## Standard limits
- \( \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\sin x}{x} = 1 \)
- \( \lim_{x \to \infty} \left(1 + \frac{1}{x}\right)^x = e \)
- \( \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{e^x - 1}{x} = 1 \)

## Techniques
- Substitute first; if indeterminate (\( 0/0 \), \( \infty/\infty \)), try factoring, rationalizing, or L'Hôpital.
